Life cycle methods in React:

THREE LIFE CYCLE METHODS IN REACT: 
        Mounting
        Updating
        Unmounting


MOUNTING:Birth stage of components
        contains 4 bulit in methods
            Constructor()
            GetDerivedFromProps()
            Render()
            ComponentDidMount()

Updating:Just Updating the components
        contains 5 built in methods
            GetDerivedFromProps()
            Render()
            ShouldComponentsUpdate()
            GetSnapShotBeforeUpdate()
            componentDidUpdate()

Unmounting:Removed from the react component 
            Components removed from DOM.Built in method is
                ComponentwillMount()
